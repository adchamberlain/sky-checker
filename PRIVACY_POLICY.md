# Privacy Policy for SkyChecker

**Last Updated: January 8, 2026**

## Overview

SkyChecker ("the App") is developed by Andrew Chamberlain. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **What:** Your device's GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude)
- **Why:** To calculate which celestial objects are visible from your specific location
- **Storage:** Location data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect personal information (name, email, etc.)
- We do not track your usage or behavior
- We do not use analytics or advertising services
- We do not share any data with third parties

## Third-Party Services

### NASA JPL Horizons API
The App connects to NASA's Jet Propulsion Laboratory Horizons System to retrieve astronomical data. When you use the App:
- Your location coordinates are sent to NASA's servers to calculate celestial object positions
- NASA's privacy policy applies to their handling of this data
- No personal identifying information is sent to NASA

For NASA's privacy policy, visit: https://www.nasa.gov/privacy/

## Data Storage

All app data, including:
- Cached astronomical calculations
- Your location preferences
- App settings

...is stored locally on your device using iOS's standard storage mechanisms. This data is not backed up to iCloud and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

## Children's Privacy

SkyChecker does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The App is suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
